What is NYC Bike Map?

NYC Bike Map is a unified, interactive bike map for NYC that combines information from NYC OpenData, Citi Bike and other sources. It is currently still under development but it currently displays the NYC Bike network, bike rack locations, bike shelter locations and Citi Bike dock locations.

Who made NYC Bike Map?

NYC Bike Map was created and is under development by Todd Lee-Millstein and Superior Labs. It is also open source so anybody is able to contribute to it if they want.
